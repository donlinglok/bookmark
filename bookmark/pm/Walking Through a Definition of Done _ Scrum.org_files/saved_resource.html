            Sai D. aldous antony a year ago

            On the one hand you have the DoD, a list of criteria to be passed.

            Then there are "All outstanding defects (or incidents such as build issues) should be
            elicited and resolved, or accepted by the team as not being
            contra-indicative to release."

            If the team decides that these outstanding defects are indeed a problem for a release, they take the unfinished list of criteria in the DoD and create a "Deficit for Release" for that item.

            This "Deficit for Release" will sharpen the DoD for the next sprint and this deficit needs to be solved in the next sprint. So, there cannot be an item in the "Deficit for Release" which is not also part of the list of criteria in the DoD!
